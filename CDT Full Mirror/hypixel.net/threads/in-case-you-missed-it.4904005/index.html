Hey there! It's been quite a year here at Hypixel, and we're glad you've stuck around with us. We have noticed that some updates will fly under the radar for our wider community and have decided to start a series of semi-regular recap posts.

This thread will cover...
  • Game Updates
  • Events
  • Bug Fixes
  • ... and everything else!
To keep reading, click Continue Reading.

► SkyBlock Updates

The Crimson Isle - Patch 0.13
Released to SkyBlock on April 20th, 2022


The Crimson Isle is the biggest update the Hypixel Team has ever worked on for SkyBlock, surpassing even the Dwarven Mines. From game designers to developers to artists to quality assurance, the team has spent countless hours working on this update to bring you the best gameplay.

This thread could never do this update justice, so if you'd like to check it out, you can view the full thread for this update HERE.

​


SkyBlock Stranded
Released to SkyBlock on January 18th, 2022

Our newest profile challenge, SkyBlock Stranded, brings all the fun of Ironman profiles while also stranding you on your private island. Are you skilled enough to progress without any outside help?

Official SkyBlock Wiki Release
Released to SkyBlock on March 10th, 2022

Have you ever wondered how often an item may drop or how a specific mechanic works in SkyBlock? We've got you covered! Take a peek at our very cool and new SkyBlock Wiki.
Check it out through the new Wiki button on the website navigation bar.

Tool Reforges Patch
Released to SkyBlock on April 1st, 2022

Brand new reforges for tools were added along with some nice QoL additions.

QoL Bug Fixes
Released to SkyBlock on April 18th, 2022

Many bugs were squashed here. Typos begone!

Rusty Unleashed
Released to SkyBlock on April 25th, 2022

This update brought about some helpful changes to a community favorite NPC, Rusty! If you've ever lost a one-time quest reward, Rusty is here to help you out.

Crimson Isle Weeks 1 & 2 Patch
Released to SkyBlock on April 29th, 2022

As we're actively monitoring the newly released island, our team crushed some bugs to make sure you nether encounter these issues again.

Crimson Isle Achievements & Bug Fixes
Released to SkyBlock on May 4th, 2022

Continuing on our quest to iron out issues, we fixed some more bugs while also introducing some achievements to gain during your Crimson Isle journey.

► Wool Wars
Released to PTL on March 15th, 2022


Wool you become the best? Our newest PVP game mode on the Hypixel Network allows for fast-paced and skilled gameplay. We've been regularly updating this mode to ensure balance while also introducing fun new maps that change the flow of battle.

Check our progress in the threads below, or hop in a game and find out!

Update v0.2 - Layout Editor, New Maps, Balance Changes + more!
Released to PTL on March 23rd, 2022

In its first patch, Wool Wars received the addition of a Layout Editor, new maps, and some class balancing.

Update v0.3 - New Maps, New Class & more
Released to PTL on March 31st, 2022

In this patch, the Builder class was added. We also added new maps, fixed some bugs, and cleaned up the statistics you receive at the end of the game.

Update v0.4 - Bug Fixes, Balance changes & more
Released to PTL on April 11th, 2022

Patch 0.4 brought many bug fixes, more balancing, and some helpful QoL additions.

Concerning Builder Kit
Released to PTL on April 25th, 2022

After reviewing some player feedback and kit selection data, we decided to retire the Builder class.

Update v0.5 - New Class & Balance Changes
Released to PTL on May 2nd, 2022

Introducing the Golem class! Gain a hefty portion of armor when activating your keystone ability. We also threw in some new maps and adjusted the Archer, Berserker, and Utility kits.

Update v0.6 - New Class & More Balance Changes
Released to PTL on May 6th, 2022

Update v0.6 brought along with it the new Engineer Class! This powerful class stops other players from breaking or placing blocks, stopping them dead in their tracks. Additionally, the Archer, Tank, Berserker, Golem, and Utility classes were all balance-tuned a bit.


► Bed Wars
Map Update

Released on May 2nd, 2022

As the name implies, this update was focused on spicing up our map pool for Bed Wars! Bringing with it 16 new maps for various playstyles and modes, we hope you find a new favorite in this bunch.


► Cops & Crims
Gun Game Update
Released March 23rd, 2022

If you're a fan of FFA game modes, Gun Game may be for you!
Each time you get a kill, you receive a new gun which you must get a kill with before moving on to the next.
In this update, we also squashed some bugs, made some QoL changes, and updated some maps.


► Smash Heroes

Smash Heroes Update
Released January 25th, 2022

New maps, some helpful QoL changes, as well as some well-needed balancing were implemented. This update was the last Smash Heros major content update we'll be releasing. This does not mean we will ever ignore the game if any game-breaking bugs were to show up.

However, new content should not be expected in the future.
​


► Tournaments

Bed Wars

Announced on March 11th, 2022
Results Announced on March 23rd, 2022

So far this year, we've hosted a Bed Wars Doubles Tournament. We'll always post tournament announcements on our main forums page (like this thread). If you've never been involved, typically our tournaments last 48 hours, where players compete to see who will come out as the best worldwide!


► Holiday Events

Lunar New Year
Released on January 28th, 2022

​

As the Lunar New Year approached, here at Hypixel we celebrated the Year of the Tiger! This event held new maps and new cosmetics for multiple game modes.

Valentine's Day
Released on February 11th, 2022

This year, we decided to keep Valentine's Day low-key, offering some handsome discounts on the Hypixel Store

(If you ever want the best deals, our in-game store also always offers a 5% discount over our web store) ​


Easter Celebration
Released on April 12th, 2022

We sprung into spring with our Easter Celebration! With this, we reskinned some lobbies, brought back Easter Simulator, introduced some new maps, and added some fun seasonal cosmetics.

Hypixel Anniversary Event
Released on April 13th, 2022

The Hypixel Network celebrated its ninth year in 2022. To celebrate, we held a week-long scavenger hunt event for some exclusive cosmetics.

We're glad to have been a part of your Minecraft experience all this time and we hope to see you next year for the tenth!

► Bug Fixes

SkyWars Bug Fixes
Released on January 20th, 2022

SkyWars received some TLC with this update, where we fixed some bugs and made some QoL changes to hopefully improve your SkyWars experience.

January Bug Fixes
Released on January 27th, 2022

As the year began, we decided to squash some of the bugs that were left after winter. We fixed some issues in...
  • The Main Lobby
  • Arcade Games
  • Bed Wars
  • Blitz Survival Games
  • Build Battle
  • Classic Games
  • Cops and Crims
  • Housing
  • ... and many more!
General February Bug Fixes
Released on February 8th, 2022

As a follow-up to the January patch, the February bug fix update brought some fixes to the following but not limited to...
  • Arcade Games
  • Build Battle
  • Mega Walls
  • SkyWars
  • Speed UHC
  • TNT Games
  • Warlord
  • Guilds / Lobbies
  • Replays
Bed Wars + Blitz Bug Fixes
Released on May 9th, 2022

This update brought the "disintegration" of a few Blitz Survival Games bugs, along with some in Bed Wars.
In addition, some general QoL changes were made in both games to make your playing experience better overall.

► General Updates

Redesigned Main Lobby
Released on April 4th, 2022

With the release of our new Main Lobby in April, we bid farewell to our previous lobby design in favor of giving it a new fresh look with lots to do! We haven't spruced up the Main Lobby since 2017, which makes this a large change for us. We hope you enjoy exploring and finding all the secrets it holds.

New Hypixel Translations
Released on March 31st, 2022

We've been working to introduce some newly translated languages onto the Hypixel Network. With this update, we added support for Ukrainian and Norwegian.

If you'd like to check these out in-game, you can find more information including how to get involved in translating Hypixel HERE.

Hypixel Rules Update
Released on April 19th, 2022

Our Hypixel Rules section received a much-needed renovation in this update.

In the future, we plan to move our SkyBlock and other rules sections to our Support Knowledge Base.

This year, we also decided to make some changes to how we word our punishments to improve clarity.

Old messages will be on the left, while the new message will be on the right.
  • Account Security Alert -> Suspicious activity has been detected on your account
  • Extreme User Disrespect -> Extreme Chat Infraction
  • Griefing -> Sabotaging the gameplay of other players on your team

Account Linking (Forums & Migration Validation)
Migration Post Released February 4th, 2022
Account Linking Post Released April 12th, 2022

Mojang accounts this year have begun the transition to Microsoft accounts, and as such, we have decided to require your account be migrated to play our competitive game modes on the Hypixel Network. This includes Ranked SkyWars, Tournaments, UHC Champions, Mega Walls, and The Pit.

If you still need to validate the status of your account, please visit our guide HERE.
(Please note, this process is unable to validate accounts purchased through Xbox Gamepass)

Also regarding Minecraft accounts, we now require a Minecraft account to be linked to your Hypixel Forums account to begin posting on our platform. Instructions on how to do so can be found HERE. More information can be found regarding this in the thread linked above.

Reporting on the Hypixel Forums
Released February 6th, 2022

In short, we added some QoL features to our forum reporting system. This includes several different preset report options, which are meant to make reporting more seamless and give a wider range of potential reasons someone was reported.

Changes to Bug Reports
Released on February 22nd, 2022

Our bug reporting section received a change-up this year. For the user (you!), the process has not changed whatsoever, with confirmation being sent by our internal systems that the bug has been logged for review. Unfortunately, this does also mean that the bug rating was removed.
In an additional attempt to make the reporting process more seamless, we overhauled the bug reporting UI, including features such as being able to select a recent game you played through our replay system!

If our team needs more information from you, we will respond to your bug report asking for additional information.

Hypixel Server Trailer
Released on January 3rd, 2022

Nothing much to say here except check it out! We hope you enjoy it.


​
Sharing Clips with Hypixel
Released on January 11th, 2022

We opened this section on the Hypixel Forums for users to submit cool and exciting moments they experience while playing on the server. These clips may be used at a later date on our Social Media platforms, so please make sure that these clips are your own work.


... And with that, thank you all for reading!

Changes to Bug Reports
Released on February 22nd, 2022

Our bug reporting section received a change-up this year. For the user (you!), the process has not changed whatsoever, with confirmation being sent by our internal systems that the bug has been logged for review. Unfortunately, this does also mean that the bug rating was removed.
In an additional attempt to make the reporting process more seamless, we overhauled the bug reporting UI, including features such as being able to select a recent game you played through our replay system!

If our team needs more information from you, we will respond to your bug report asking for additional information.
