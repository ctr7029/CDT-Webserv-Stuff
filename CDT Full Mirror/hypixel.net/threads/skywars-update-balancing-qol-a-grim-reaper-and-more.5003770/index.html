SkyWars Update - Balancing, QoL, a Grim Reaper and more!

  • Thread starter Brandonjja
  • Start date Aug 9, 2022

Hello!

Today we're bringing you a new update for SkyWars. With this update comes a lot of Balancing and Quality of Life changes, new Mythical Kits, a Grim Reaper, and more!

Read below for more details!

► General Changes
After several discussions with Game Designers and the Hypixel Player Council, it was decided to start cutting back on the many currencies in SkyWars. These currencies ended up adding too much complexity to SkyWars for newcomers and it didn't make sense to keep so many around. Moving forward, shards will no longer be obtainable. Gathering souls will replace their purpose of progress towards opals. The number of souls needed to obtain an opal will be significantly lower than the previous number of shards required, therefore we will be resetting any shard progress players currently have and granting anyone level 10 and above a free opal as compensation.

Any perks that increased how many shards you gained, such as Shard Seeker from the Angel's Descent, will now be applied to Souls instead. We have also removed some requirements needed to earn progress toward an opal, such as needing to max out the Harvesting Season perk and needing to keep your soul inventory full. The requirement of needing to be gold prestige remains.

After looking at our stats for lab modes, it was also decided to make most modes only available through private party games. Lucky Mode will now have its own NPC, being the most popular of the lab modes, and will always remain available. Mega Mode will be removed and made available as a weekend special, only appearing on Saturday and Sunday between 12pm and 6pm EST.

► Grim Reaper
The Grim Reaper is a new NPC that will be used for late-game progression content such as the Angel's Descent, Angel of Death, Head Collection, and the Angel's Brewery.



This allows the Soul Well menu to return to basic soul-related purposes only such as soul harvesting and soul upgrading.

► Angel's Brewery
Players at least diamond prestige and above will now have access to the Angel's Brewery - a place to brew Potions that give a variety of perks and bonuses for each game they're active. Each time you purchase a Potion you will be able to use it for 25 game wins (purchasing the same Potion multiple times stacks how many uses it has and will only be consumed in games you win). You may buy several types of Potions at the same time, but you can only have 1 active at any moment. Use the bottom row of the menu to choose which Potion you want active, or disable them and save them for another time!



If you have a Potion selected, it will automatically be applied in the next game you play, giving you all the perks and bonuses of your selected Potion.

► New Mythical Kits

Chronobreaker
  • "Echo" back in time, teleport to the position you were standing 10 seconds ago.
  • Heal 2 hearts of your current health when you Echo.


Cryomancer
  • Right-click with a sword to permanently turn the ground around you into ice.
  • Starts with 1 Ice Bridge Egg and 16 snowballs.


► Perk Balancing
Moving forward, rather than being able to equip every perk you own all at once, players will now have to select up to 6 perks to be active at a time. Players can change which perks are active at any time in the SkyWars lobby and may choose to have less than 6 perks active if desired. This change was made with the intent of helping new players have a better chance at fighting seasoned veterans, while giving the more senior players a way to create new strategies based on which perks they choose to activate.

Your currently active perks will appear in the middle of the new perks menu for each mode. If you have less than 6 perks currently active, a blank slot will appear indicating you're able to equip extra perks.



In addition to the following perk balance changes, all tiered perks will now only have a single tier.
  • Arrow Recovery chance increased from 25% to 50%
  • Blazing Arrows chance increased from 5% to 15%
  • Mining Expertise will now always give 1 extra ore
  • Speed Boost duration increased to 5 minutes
  • Speed Boost will now give Haste 2 instead of Haste 1
  • Revenge chance significantly increased
  • Bridger chance increased from 25% to 50%
  • Environmental Expert damage reduction increased to 50%
  • Necromancer chance increased from 8% to 16%
  • Black Magic chance increased from 15% to 30%
  • Robbery chance increased from 10% to 20%
  • Frost chance increased from 20% to 40%
  • Barbarian will now give sharpness every 3 axe kills rather than being based on chance
  • Savior can now be enabled in all modes
  • Bulldozer can now be enabled in all modes
  • Ender Mastery has been removed as a perk and added as a default feature for all players
  • Instant Smelting has been removed as a perk and added as a default feature for all players
  • Meticulous Miner is now an upgrade for Mining Expertise, granting a 20% chance for triple drops
  • Fireproof is now an upgrade for Nourishment, granting fire resistance for 5s for tier 1, 7s for tier 2, and 9s for tier 3
  • Apothecary now increases potion durations by 30% rather than 10%
  • Librarian now give an enchantment book every 3 kills rather than having a 10% chance per kill
  • Smarty Pants is now an upgrade for Mining Expertise
  • Telekinesis is now an upgrade for Speed Boost and works on all blocks rather than just ores

► New SkyWars Lobby
SkyWars now has a new lobby! Take some time to explore!



► New Achievements
  • Ender Party - Have a corrupted, time warp, and normal ender pearl in your inventory at the same time (5 AP).
  • Skewered! - Kill a player by pricking them to death with a cactus (10 AP).
  • Tonic Tanker (tiered) - Brew the Angel's Brewery (1, 5, 25, 50, 100) times (5, 10, 15, 20, 25 AP).
► Quality of Life Changes
  • If your soul inventory is full you will now gain coins for each kill.
  • Harvesting Season perk now has an additional 1% chance to get double souls per tier.
  • Corrupted games now give souls as well as heads.
  • Level requirements for the Angel's Descent main branches are removed (side branches still have level requirements).
  • Perks in the Angel's Descent can now be toggled by right-clicking the perk.
  • All perks in the Angel's Descent now show a description regardless if they are locked or unlocked.
  • Angel's Descent purchases will now display a confirmation menu to prevent accidental purchases.
  • Opals will now be rewarded for each level gained between levels 11 and 19.
  • Midas Gift perk now grants 20 extra coins per lucky kill per tier.
  • Midas Gift perk now gives an extra soul on lucky kills.
► New Maps
New maps have been added in for Solo/Doubles!
