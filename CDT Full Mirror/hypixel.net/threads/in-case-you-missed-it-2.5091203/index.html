Hey! We're back again as Summer comes to a close to recap everything 'Hypixel' that has happened over the past few months. If you missed the last thread, no worries, it can be found HERE.

This thread will cover:
  • Game Updates
  • Events
  • Bug Fixes
  • ... and everything else!
To keep reading, click Continue Reading.

âº SkyBlock Updates

The Sunshade Patch
Released to SkyBlock on May 11th, 2022

Nothing says summer quite like shades, right? In this update, we introduced a new talisman to finally get those pesky Dive Ghasts to leave you alone, gave some increased visibility to various items and stats, as well as a handful of other adjustments that can be viewed in the linked thread.

Auction House Maximum Bid Limit Increase
Released to SkyBlock on July 28th, 2022

In making some upgrades to our backend systems, we were finally able to graduate from the 32-bit integer limitation for auction house bids. This does mean that since July, and going forward, the new limit on purchasing items will be capped at 10 billion. Happy bidding!

The Thunder Revolution + QOL
Released to SkyBlock on August 1st, 2022

In collaboration with our SkyBlock Nerds, we brought this 'shocking' update to a SkyBlock server near you! We introduced some new methods to charge thunder bottle items, making the process way more interactive and seamless. In other news, it is also much easier to pick up items dropped from miniboss/boss loot and won't require a constant shuffle for them to be picked up.

The Chili Pepper Update
Released to SkyBlock on August 5th, 2022

Some think spice is nice, while others may think it's complete gabagool. This update introduced a whole new collection with some very unique recipes and enchants to help those using inferno minions. Some helpful new bits shop items were also added, bugs were squashed, and some balancing was done for various items such as sorrow armor and the wisp pet.

Shen's Auctions, The Hex, RNG Meter, and more!
Released to SkyBlock on August 18th, 2022

Ever get overwhelmed with making sure your item is the best it can be? No worries, the Hex is here to make enchanting and modifying your gear a breeze. With this update, enchantments were also added to the bazaar along with other various improvements, like a new search feature to make finding your desired item even easier. In other news, stats are easier to view with an improved menu, Shen's Auctions were introduced for the richest of the rich, and RNG is becoming, well, less random!

​
1.0 Design Thread - RNG and Combat
Posted to the SkyBlock Forums on August 18th, 2022

As briefly mentioned above, we're looking to revamp how randomness is treated in SkyBlock. One of the issues we're aiming to tackle with the 1.0 version of SkyBlock is the player experience when you're attempting to get a rare drop, but your luck just isn't there. Hopefully, this concept helps improve the playing experience, as being unlucky is never fun. If you have feedback, feel free to drop a comment on the thread!

Maddox is Mad
Released to SkyBlock on August 27th, 2022

To help future-proof content, the way necromancy works has been adjusted. In layman's terms, the souls stored in necromancy items now take damage from being within 12 blocks of a slayer. If you'd like to see the breakdown of how the new math works out, check out the link above.

Moby's Fishing Speed Chumpdate
Released to SkyBlock on September 6th, 2022

Bringing with it a handful of new fishing-focused content, the newest NPC Moby is here to make a 'splash'. Chum was introduced to make fishing with groups a much more collaborative experience. This update also brought a rework to the ammonite pet, new equipment, and other balancing changes. Make your way to the Mushroom Desert Island to find Moby and learn the secrets of chum!

​
Test of Control - SkyBlock Dojo Challenge
Released to SkyBlock on September 8th, 2022

Dojo Master Tao from the Crimson Isle finally decided to release a new challenge, the Test of Control. Climb the challenge leaderboard to prove you are the best! Alongside this challenge being released, some bugs were also fixed.


âº Wool Wars

Wool Wars 1.0 Release
Released on May 11th, 2022

After an ongoing presence in the PTL lobby, we decided it was finally time for a 1.0 release of Wool Wars! With this update, we added it to its very own unique lobby and added new maps, leaderboards, leveling, and more! If you're feeling competitive, try it out for a unique take on team-based PVP combat.

âº Pixel Party

Pixel Party v1.0 - Release to Arcade Games
Released on September 6th, 2022

To celebrate Pixel Party finally moving to the arcade lobby, the 1.0 release included a bunch of new features to spice up your gameplay. The new 'Hyper Mode' comes with crazy amounts of new power-ups, events, and overall chaos. To make the game more accessible, we also added colorblind settings to make sure everyone can play the game without difficulties.
Pixel Party also now features music made by ShinkoNet! Check it out on YouTube below, or in-game.


âº Housing

Housing Update - Conditions, Item Actions, and more!
Released on May 16th, 2022

Housing received some major features with this update, some being from community suggestions! You can now have a much more interactive experience developing your own housing experience with conditions, new item actions, and persistent stats. Custom commands have been greatly improved, alongside other QoL additions, to give a better experience for our creative housing community.

âº SkyWars

Removal of Ranked SkyWars
Posted June 15th, 2022

Being in an odd state for quite some time, we decided it was finally time to say goodbye to Ranked Skywars. As of 8/1/22, the game mode is officially removed and is no longer playable. Thank you to everyone who competed along the way through many seasons.



Balancing, QoL & The Grim Reaper
Released on August 9th, 2022

This update for SkyWars brought some changes to spice up the gameplay experience. We introduced the Angel's Brewery to make grinding more rewarding, added some new mythical tier kits, balanced some kits and perks, and unveiled a new SkyWars lobby! We also decided it would be a good time to add some new maps into the pool to make sure the SkyWars experience stays fresh.

Update Follow-up Patch
Posted September 1st, 2022

Following the previous update, we adjusted some changes we made to the Angel's Decent from the community feedback we received. We also did some minor balancing changes in regards to the Angel's Brewery and perks, as well as fixed various bugs.

âº Dropper


PTL Release
Released to PTL on July 25th, 2022
From our Admin Game Jam last year, we bring you Dropper! The newest addition to our prototype games lobby brings a fun mix of casual and competitive gameplay as you race to complete maps before your opponents.
Drop into the PTL lobby to give it a try!

âº VampireZ

Balances, Bug Fixes, and more!
Released on June 28th, 2022

This update brought some major backend changes to VampireZ, alongside changes for both the survivors and vampires. Hopefully, with these changes, the general playing experience should feel much more balanced and less sluggish as we also improved shops and menus with new items, as well as not making them close after a single purchase. We also decided to cap the max party size at five players in an attempt to combat very large vampire and survivor parties. If you're like an in-depth read on what all was changed, check the thread above for all those juicy details.
Happy (vampire) hunting!

âº Tournaments

Mini Walls
Announced on August 5th, 2022
Results Announced on August 19th, 2022

Our first go at a Mini Walls tournament happened this August. Thank you to all who competed in 4 years and 6 months of combined playtime. We'll always post tournament announcements on our main forums page (like this thread). If you've never been involved, typically, our tournaments last 48 hours, where players compete to see who will come out as the best worldwide!

âº Holiday Events

Summer Event 2022
Released on June 23rd, 2022

This summer event brought with it some major changes to how we'll be doing future seasonal content. We want to move away from randomized loot crates, and towards a more progression-based system, such as our new Event Silver, which allows you to get the cosmetics you want in the order you want!
As usual, we also had a store sale, as well as some cool new maps and a reskinned main lobby to really get those summer vibes in session.

âº Bug Fixes

SkyBlock Bug Fixes
Released June 22nd, 2022
Released July 27th, 2022
Released on August 19th, 2022
Released on August 23rd, 2022
Released on August 27th, 2022

SkyBlock went through many rounds of bug fixes throughout the past few months, ironing out hopefully many of the issues that are causing some trouble. Although there are too many to list here, the various patch notes can be found above detailing everything that was squashed.

Various Bug Fixes
Released on June 28th, 2022

In a more general network-focused bug fix patch, we conquered some bugs in the following game modes:
  • Arcade Games
  • Bed Wars
  • Blitz Survival Games
  • Build Battle
  • Mega Walls
  • Murder Mystery
  • Quakecraft
  • Replays
  • SkyWars
  • TNT Games
  • VampireZ
  • Walls
  • and Warlords
As a reminder, bugs and exploits can be reported on the Hypixel Forum's Server Bug Reports section using the reporting form found HERE.

âº General Updates

1.19 Support
Released on June 7th, 2022

This update is short and sweet; we've introduced 1.19 Minecraft Support to the Hypixel Network. This does extend to SMPs, so if you want to play on the latest version, you can start a new server or update your existing world from your control panel.

Chat Filter Changes
Released on July 25th, 2022

We've decided that our previous profanity filters could be a bit unclear to players, as we don't explicitly state what falls under each category. Instead, we've moved towards a system shown below. In addition, we revamped the chat filter of blocked phrases and increased the /myfilter command limit from 16 to 50 words.


​
New Report Option - Signs/Holograms
Released on September 6th, 2022

We've added a new option to the /report book menu called Signs/Holograms. This allows you to report inappropriate signs or holograms across our server, like in Housing or SkyBlock. This new option will notify you to use our new command, /worldreport. You don't even need to know who specifically made the sign or hologram! You just need to use the new /worldreport command while looking at a sign or hologram, and our systems will take care of the rest.


SkyBlock Rules & Policies Changes
Released on May 19th, 2022

We've revamped our SkyBlock Rules to be more in line with our regular Server Rules. This came with a much-needed visual facelift, alongside some additional clarification on various aspects of disallowed behavior. Check it out at hypixel.net/rules!

Removal of Microsoft Account Migration Requirement
Changed July 12th, 2022

As migration appears to have been completed, we decided to remove the requirement on competitive games that required your account to be validated as migrated. This applies to all competitive games on the network.

Dev Blog - Optimizing Reports with Machine Learning
Released on August 29th, 2022

Our newest Dev blog comes brought to you by our Administrator Haylee (aka inventivetalent). This blog goes incredibly in-depth about how our systems are utilizing machine learning to optimize the reporting process. If you've ever wondered why using /report is helpful to us, this thread is definitely worth a read.

Blocking of Websites & Other Domains
Changed August 14th, 2022

As of the previously mentioned date, we will be blocking all non-whitelisted website URLs or other domains from being mentioned anywhere on the server. This will include party chat, guild chat, private messages, and all the other systems. Hopefully, these changes are able to keep you and your account as safe as possible while playing on the network.
Please also make sure to continue reporting any potential scams or phishing attempts via our /report command, and our moderation team will take care of them as quickly as possible.

SkyBlock Nerds Applications
Applications Closed July 24th, 2022

Although SkyBlock Nerds applications are currently closed, we recently brought some fresh faces into our Discord. If you've ever felt passionate about SkyBlock game design, I would keep an eye out when applications open in the future.

In Memoriam: Technoblade
Announced on July 1st, 2022

In love for Technoblade, we brought the community together to leave messages for his family. The book and messages are still in the works of being moderated and printed as there were far more messages than we could have anticipated. We will keep you posted on how this is going when we can.

Thank you all for reading!
