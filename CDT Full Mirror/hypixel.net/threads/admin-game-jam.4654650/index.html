Hey everyone!

A couple weeks ago, the Admin team did their semi-annual Game Jam and this time, we decided to post about what came out of it! For those that don't know, a Game Jam is when we give a week to the Admin team to develop whatever they want. From games to features, you name it. This time, we have quite a few games that the team did in that week:
  • Cops and Crims - Gun Game
  • SheepWars
  • The Dropper
  • Hypixel Lab
  • Pixel Party
  • Gliders
Interested about what these projects looked like? Read on to find out more!

â¶ Gun Game
Gun Game is a classic shooting minigame where you show off your skills and get a kill with every gun in the game! With each kill, your gun changes to the next weapon tier. To win, you have to get the first kill with the last tier: the knife. With a speed boost, jump boost, and damage boost to the knife, it certainly is a threat to contend with in the endgame stage.

For the showcase, we used maps already in the game. For this, we used Alleyway!


Wow, I'm in second place.


Never mind...


â¶ SheepWars
Check out this explosive fun time, SheepWars! Two teams face each other with different kinds of, you guessed it, exploding sheep to see who is the last one standing. The islands are not connected, so you have to rely on the knockback from the explosions to defeat your opponents.

The Rules board is a tad bit confusing, but we got the hang of it.


All the different kinds of sheep keeps you on your toes to make sure you don't go flying!


Check out the different sheep effects!


â¶ The Dropper
A classic in Minecraft minigames, the Dropper was recreated in this Game Jam! As the name suggest, the goal of the game is to drop through different and complex zones to land in the water. Hitting something will cause you to restart the level, so fall carefully!

Jump in!


Try not to hit anything!


â¶ Hypixel Lab
Spooky times ahead with the Hypixel Lab! In this maze of a lab, there are three groups of players: Monsters, Prisoners and Swat.

The Monsters, though there are only a few, are scary creatures who are working to eliminate all other players. They are strong, but all come with various quirks, like being unable to move when spotted or not seeing people standing still.


Prisoners work to escape from the confusing underground maze to arrive at the surface while hopefully avoiding the Monsters.


The Swat team's goal is to eliminate the OP Monsters and protect themselves and Prisoners.


â¶ Pixel Party
In case you haven't seen, Pixel Party was already released to the Prototype Lobby!


Read more about it here > https://hypixel.net/threads/new-ptl-game-pixel-party.4604508/

â¶ Gliders
Gliders is a special treat for all of us since it was a showcase of what some of our developers could do in a week with 1.17! Gliders is a game where you race against other teams to make it to the goal via the air. While this game is a good way for us to see the variety of things we could do with 1.17, it would require extensive network changes to put it on the server, so don't expect this to be a release anytime soon.



The race course consists of red rings that players need to fly through that act as checkpoints if you fall and are required to make it to the goal, so keep on the path!


If you find yourself falling, different arrow boosters can help lift you up to keep you in the air!


While not implemented in the Game Jam, coins are meant to be collected during the race that could be used to buy cosmetics, etc.


â¶ To Wrap This All Up...
Sadly, not all of these games will make it to the PTL, but we are going to be trying to bring a couple of them online! Like Gliders, some of these games are a bit technically difficult to add to the server or need some big bug fixes and feature changes, so you might not see these on the live network for a while, if ever. What was your favourite Game Jam Game we showcased? Any of them you'd love to play on the Hypixel Server?

And a final note, enjoy the ez spam of some toxic GMs and Admins...

Hypixel Lab: @Nitroholic_, @Plummel, and @Cheesey
