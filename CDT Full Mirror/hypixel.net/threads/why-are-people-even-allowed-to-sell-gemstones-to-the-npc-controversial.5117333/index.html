Ever since gemstones were introduced, it has always been the meta to sell to NPCs for more profit per gemstone than at the bazaar. However, this has resulted in massive inflation due to how profitable gemstone mining was. My question is: why was it even allowed in the first place?
1. Pristine is very overpowered
Admins designed this skill with the intent of making gemstone mining a viable moneymaking method. Why they didn't factor in NPC sell prices originally (base gemstones were EIGHT coins per) is beyond me.
2. It would reduce inflation and stop dupes
I've already brought up how it caused a lot of inflation and continues to. However, the admins (in trying to stop dupes) made perfect/flawless gems unsellable to NPCs. This is a good change. I think that this should also be applied to the other tiers. Of course, there would be exceptions for ironman players.

Sorry if this was a little short, I am a little frustrated that this is happening.
