Pixel Party v1.0 - Release to Arcade Games

  • Thread starter Hypixel Team
  • Start date Sep 6, 2022

You should upgrade or use an alternative browser.
  • Sep 6, 2022
  • Replies: 134


Hello!

Today we're releasing an update for Pixel Party with a new mode, new floors, new power-ups, colorblind mode, and more! From now on, Pixel Party will be playable through the Arcade Lobby. With this, we're also including cosmetics, achievements, and other goodies that come with Arcade Games.

Check it out in the Arcade Lobby!

► Hyper Mode
Hyper Mode takes Pixel Party to the next level with several events designed to cause some chaos. They're meant to throw you for a loop, splash colors everywhere, and sometimes, maybe help you out a little! Oh and of course, more power-ups spawn!




​
► Colorblind Settings
With Pixel Party's full release, we've included new colorblind options so anyone can play the game without difficulties! We have included a range of predefined options, however players can also fully customize their own color scheme as desired. You can find these options in the Pixel Party NPC in the Arcade Lobby!



► Music
Pixel Party now features music made by ShinkoNet! You can control the volume in-game and the music will stop when the floor drops!


► Achievements
We've added new achievements to Pixel Party for a total of 190 Achievement Points!
  • Clumsy Dancer - 5 Achievement Points
    • Fall into the void while the floor is still filled in
  • Silent Disco- 5 Achievement Points
    • Turn off the music
  • Expert Dancer - 15 Achievement Points
    • Reach round 25 in Normal Mode
  • Saving Throw - 10 Achievement Points
    • Save yourself from falling with an Ender Pearl after round 15
  • Crushed the Floor - 5 Achievement Points
    • Get crushed by an anvil in Hyper Mode
  • Color Coordinated - 5/10/15/20/25 Achievement Points
    • Survive 25/100/250/1000/2000 rounds in Pixel Party
  • Powered Up- 5/10/15/20/25 Achievement Points
    • Collect 5/15/25/50/150 Power-Ups in Pixel Party Normal Mode

► Other Updates
  • Overall game speed has been increased (earlier rounds are slightly quicker, waiting periods have been slightly reduced, etc.)
  • Added new cosmetics: Party Pants and Disco Helmets!
  • Added new power-ups:
    • Color Trail
    • Magic Carpet
    • Paint-filled Egg
    • Speed Boost
    • No Hunger
    • Random Glass Boxes
    • Crowd
  • Added new floors.
  • Arcade Coins, Hypixel Experience, Guild Experience, and (when applicable) Event Experience are now rewarded.
  • Added a Pixel Party Challenge in the Quest Master.
  • Added Pixel Party stats to the Arcade Stats NPC and /stats.

We hope you enjoy these updates to Pixel Party, head over to the Arcade Lobby to check it out!
