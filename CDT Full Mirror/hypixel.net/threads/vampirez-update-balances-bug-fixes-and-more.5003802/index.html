VampireZ Update - Balances, Bug Fixes and more!

  • Thread starter Nitroholic_
  • Start date Jun 28, 2022

  • Jun 28, 2022
  • Replies: 187


Hey all, today we are rolling a balance patch for VampireZ, available to play now in the Classic Lobby. Read on below for a full list of changes and additions or play it right now by clicking the Classic Lobby NPC, game menu icon, or using the command /lobby classic.

► What is in this update?
  • Balances
  • Lobby Improvements
  • Revamped Survivor Shop
  • Bug Fixes and QOL Changes
  • And more!
Please note that this patch contains a considerable amount of rewriting of the backend systems, which may result in some unintentional bugs being brought in, so please report them in the Bugs Section of the forums and we will look into them ASAP.


► General Changes
  • Added a max party limit of 5.
    • We felt it was necessary to introduce a party limit to tackle large Vampire and Survivor parties. We will monitor this change and adjust the limit as necessary.
  • Added a Human Kills leaderboard to the lobby, accessible via the leaderboard cycler.
  • Rewrote the lobby shop to improve the user experience.
    • This change includes displays for when you can afford perk upgrades, indicators when you have maxed a perk, not closing the menu after each upgrade purchase etc.
  • Lengthened the starting grace period from 19->20 seconds.
  • Added new rainbow colored chat prefixes for 15k+ Survivor Wins and 100k+ Survivor Kills.
  • Added total coins transferred in the current match at the bottom of the currency conversion menu icons.
  • Converting coins to currency will no longer automatically close the menu, making it easier to make multiple purchases at once.
  • Menu elements in both the Survivor and Vampire shop now show if you can currently afford the item or not.
    • The menu will also automatically update cleanly without flashing when you gain currency with it open.
  • Lowered the AFK Timeout period down to 3 minutes.
  • The Magic Books and their underlying system has been improved, making them easier to use and more balanced.
    • This change includes improved gear for visibility, tweaked effect radii, and increased casting times which makes decisions on when to cast and when to cancel casting to switch gear more consequential.
  • Both teams scoreboards will now display a counter of the number of members on that team, as well as the number of players on the opposing team.
  • The color of the Survivor, Vampire, and Zombie counts on the scoreboard has been changed to aqua to help them stand out more.


► Survivor Changes
This update contains a decent amount of Survivor balance changes, including an overhaul of the Survivor Shop and its contents, tweaks to the starting gear, and other miscellaneous changes.


The new Survivor Shop.​

Shop Changes
  • (-) The plain Iron Sword has been removed.
    • All Survivors will now be spawning with a plain Iron Sword by default, therefore it is no longer needed in the shop.
  • (-) Hardened Sprung Steel Sword, Plain Leather Armor, and Cow Out food set have been removed.
    • To consolidate the items in the Survivor Shop menu and also free up some space, we decided to remove some of the items which we decided were less valuable in the game or that players very rarely purchased.
  • (-) The Regeneration Potion has been removed from the Strength Potion combo.
  • (-) The Book of Strength has been removed.
    • This items ability in its current state was simply far too strong and unbalanced.
  • (+) Added a new Holy Book: Fortune's Favor (1000 Gold, 60s Cast Time, 8 Block Radius).
    • Fortunes Favor will provide all nearby players with an additional +10 Gold per Vampire kill and +2 Gold per Zombie Kill at the cost of a high price and a hefty initial cast period.
  • (+) Added a new Item: Blessed Amulet (100 Gold).
    • The Blessed Amulet will increase your chance of acquiring loot drops from Zombie kills by +15% while held in your inventory (stacks with the Loot Drops perk). This item's effect does not stack more than once.
  • (~) The Healing Holy Book's (Now Healing Cleric) cast time has been increased from 10->30 seconds and the cost increased from 400->500 Gold.
  • (~) The 45s Regeneration Potion has been increased to 2 minutes and its price has been changed from 50->75 Gold.
    • We decided to make these 2 changes to balance the potency of the Strength Potion set and make the Regeneration Potion (now Rejuvenation Tonic) more viable. We still plan to revisit the Strength Potion and make more changes if necessary in the future.
  • (~) Both purchasable Diamond Swords have been given a buy limit of 2.
    • In order to bring this in line with the Diamond Armors buy limit of 3, we thought it was necessary to introduce a buy limit for the swords as well.
  • (~) Most items and weapons have been given better, more lore appropriate names.
  • (~) The default Leather Armor color has been changed from the vanilla brown to navy blue.
Other Changes
  • All Survivors will now spawn with an Iron Sword instead of a Stone Sword at the start of the match.
  • The last 3 remaining Survivors will now be given Regeneration II instead of Damage Resistance II.
  • Removed the ability to find a stack of 10 Holy Waters in Loot Chests.
  • Transfusion Perk chances reduced from 10->35->75% to 10->25->50%.
  • Purchases in the Survivor Shop will no longer automatically close the menu, making it easier to make multiple purchases at once.
  • Removed the spammy cooldown message when eating a Golden Apple.
  • Made some improvements to the tutorial messaging for new players.
  • All magic casters will now have a percentage chance to gain +1 Coin and Gold when a Survivor they are affecting gets a Vampire or Zombie kill.
    • This system has been in the game for some time but seems to have not been working correctly.


► Vampire Changes
  • The Frankenstein's Monster Perk Pigman will now spawn with a full set of Golden Armor and a Sharpness I Golden Sword.
  • The Vampiric Minion Perk Wither Skeleton will now spawn with a Power I Bow.
  • Terror Level Perk % chance reduced from 10->20->30% to 5->10->15%.
  • Renamed "Break Glass" ability to "Shatter" and lowered the cooldown from 15->10 seconds.
  • Removed the annoying "YOU DIED" message from displaying every time you died as a Vampire after your first initial death.


► Bug Fixes
  • Fixed several typos in the Survivor and Vampire shop menus.
  • Improved Vampire role selection logic which should alleviate matches not starting with 3 Vampires.
  • Fixed a bug which caused all player deaths to be delayed by 5 ticks.
    • I believe this may be the cause of what many players refer to as the "health" or "healing" bug.
  • Fixed Holy Armor using your Leather Armor color preference instead of its designated color.
  • Fixed one player's Leather Armor color preference being applied to everyone's shop after they purchased it.
  • Fixed the scoreboard header saying HYPIXEL instead of VAMPIREZ.
  • Added additional checks in to show the accurate amount of zombies after a game has ended.
  • Trouble the Cat has been completely rewritten and now actually works! Zombies in an 8 block radius will aggro on Trouble, and after a short period she will explode. The explosion will no longer damage other Survivors.
  • Fixed the Diamond Leggings from Loot Chests never appearing.
