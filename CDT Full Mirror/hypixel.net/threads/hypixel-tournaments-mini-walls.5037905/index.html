Hello everyone,

We are happy to announce the next Hypixel Tournament - Mini Walls!
If you don't know about tournaments or the Tournament Hall, or if you'd like to know more about some of the terms in this post, make sure to take a look at the first tournament post.

If you missed out on previous tournaments, no big deal!
You don't need to have played in previous ones to participate. You are on an even footing in every tournament.

âº How to participate?
The Mini Walls Tournament will take place on August 12.
It will last 48 hours total, from 6:00 PM EDT August 12 to 6:00 PM EDT August 14.

âº Tournament Rules
Brackets & Points
There are 5 brackets for this tournament: Starter, Gold, Diamond, Master, and Grandmaster. As with previous tournaments, brackets serve for matchmaking. In higher brackets, you will play against better players, but also earn more points!

For this tournament, you will be able to earn points by winning, final kills, and doing the most wither damage (as a team). Points are shared with the whole team, if one player earns a final kill all players on the team will receive the points.

Every win will promote you and your team to the next bracket, however, each bracket has a minimum amount of points per game that you need to earn to stay in it, not reaching this amount of points will place you in the previous bracket.

Note: Bracket points may be tweaked before the start of the tournament.

Starter Bracket:
Win: +10 points
Final Kill: +2 points
Highest Wither Damage (as a team): +2 points
Win: Instant promotion to Gold Bracket
Anything Else: Stay in Starter Bracket.
-----------

Gold Bracket:
Win: +15 points
Final Kill: +3 points
Highest Wither Damage (as a team): +4 points
Win: Instant promotion to Diamond Bracket
Your team earned at least 3 points: You stay in Gold bracket
Your team earned 2 points or less: Demotion to Starter Bracket
-----------

Diamond Bracket:
Win: +25 points
Final Kill: +4 points
Highest Wither Damage (as a team): +6 points
Win: Instant promotion to Master Bracket
Your team earned at least 10 points: You stay in Diamond Bracket
Your team earned 9 points or less: Demotion to Gold Bracket
-----------

Master Bracket:
Win: +35 points
Final Kill: +6 points
Highest Wither Damage (as a team): +9 points
Win: Instant promotion to Grandmaster Bracket
Your team earned at least 21 points: You stay in Master Bracket
Your team earned 20 points or less: Demotion to Diamond Bracket
-----------

Grandmaster Bracket:
Win: +60 points
Final Kill: +10 points
Highest Wither Damage (as a team): +18 points
Your team earned at least 46 points: You stay in Grandmaster Bracket
Your team earned 45 points or less: Demotion to Master Bracket

Playtime + Max Games
The tournament lasts for 48 hours. As with previous tournaments, there is a limit to the maximum amount of games you can play. All players are able to play up to 50 games in the tournament. A game counts towards this limit as soon as the game starts.

For this tournament, we have also implemented automatic team balancing based on parties. This means the team selector items will be unavailable and teams will be automatically created similar to other games.

âº Tournament Rewards
Tributes
Tributes are the rewards currency of tournaments.

Your first win will grant +20 Tributes.
Each win after will reward +10 Tributes.
You can gain up to 100 tributes during the tournament.

There are 3 new items in the Tribute Shop for this tournament:
  • Mini Walls Tournament Banner
  • Mini Me (Mini Walls Cosmetic)
    ​
  • Green Rings Particle Pack
    ​

Leaderboard rewards
Tournament Points are the leaderboard currency.
Ranking on the global leaderboard will earn you huge bonus rewards:

Top 1-4: 450 Tributes, 10,000,000 Arcade coins, Blaze Pet, 6 Crafted Mystery Boxes
Top 5-8: 400 Tributes, 7,500,000 Arcade coins, 6 Crafted Mystery Boxes
Top 9-12: 350 Tributes, 5,000,000 Arcade coins, 6 Crafted Mystery Boxes
Top 13-20: 300 Tributes, 3,000,000 Arcade coins, 3 Crafted Mystery Boxes
Top 50: 250 Tributes, 2,000,000 Arcade coins, 3 Crafted Mystery Boxes
Top 100: 200 Tributes, 1,000,000 Arcade coins, 3 Crafted Mystery Boxes
Top 500: 150 Tributes, 500,000 Arcade coins, 1 random Crafted Mystery Box
Top 1000: 100 Tributes, 250,000 Arcade coins, 1 random Crafted Mystery Box
Top 5000: 50 Tributes
You will only match one of those categories (top 1 does NOT add up all the ones below)
We may adjust these values at the end of the tournament based on final placements and total participation. Rewards will never get worse for a given position.
