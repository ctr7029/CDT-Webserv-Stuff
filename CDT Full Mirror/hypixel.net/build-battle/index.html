Build Battle

In Hypixel Build Battle, players face off against each other to see who can create the best build based off of a theme. Discuss the Hypixel Build Battle game mode with other players in this forum!

Casual Games

Play featured video

In Build Battle, players face off against each other to see who can create the best build based off a theme. Build as quickly as you can before the voting starts to get as many points from other players as possible. Build Battle also includes other modes like Guess the Build, where you have to be quick and creative to try to guess what other players are building.

Build Battle is perfect for any players who love building or creating things in-game.
